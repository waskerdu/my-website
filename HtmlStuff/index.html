CINDER

CINDER is a fast paced first person shooter rougelike.

Features Include

  • Bleeding edge graffix!!!11!!1!! Like almost Wolfenstein 3D level...
  • Procedural level generation! Do you like to wander around massive empty rooms and corridors? Of course you do!
  • Enemies that can spin and you can walk straight through and don't do anything!
  • A yawning black abyss above and below you!
  • Hyper advanced lighting! (Stuff that is further from you is darker!)

Features not (yet) included

  • Normal mapping!
  • Less barbaric lighting system!
  • Guns!
  • Being able to shoot things!
  • Heads up display!
  • Floors and ceilings?
  • And most important of all: head bobbing!
